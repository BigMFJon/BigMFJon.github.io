My story

across borders

I grew up in a small village in Uzbekistan, where I did not see myself settling for what is given but couldn't have even imagined that I will potentially will be part of greatest program (IDDP). I promised myself I would one day see the places I read about in weekly journals. Persistence made it happen and there is final step to my biggest dream so far IDDP.
